PartsMtaani is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it is handled.

01

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal information is Parts Mtaani, a business registered in Kenya (Tax PIN: P051234567Z). You can reach us at orders@partsmtaani.com or on 011 987 3011.

02

Data We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when submitting a pre-order, contacting us, or otherwise engaging with this portal:

CategoryExamples
Identity dataFull name
Contact dataPhone number, email address, delivery location
Vehicle dataMake, model, year, engine code, VIN / chassis number
Order dataPart descriptions, OEM numbers, condition preference, budget, urgency
Payment dataM-Pesa transaction references (we do not store card details)
Usage dataIP address, browser type, pages visited (via analytics)

03

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data only for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Processing and fulfilling your pre-order
  • Communicating order status, quotes and delivery updates
  • Processing payments and maintaining transaction records
  • Responding to customer support enquiries
  • Complying with legal and tax obligations
  • Improving our portal and services through aggregated analytics

We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

04

Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, we rely on the following lawful bases:

  • Contract performance — processing your pre-order and communicating order updates.
  • Legal obligation — maintaining records required by KRA and other regulators.
  • Legitimate interests — fraud prevention, service security and analytics, where these do not override your rights.
  • Consent — any marketing communications, which you may withdraw at any time.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We share your data only where necessary:

  • Suppliers — vehicle and part details are shared with vetted suppliers to fulfil your order.
  • Logistics partners — your name and delivery address are shared with delivery providers.
  • Payment processors — transaction references are shared with Safaricom for M-Pesa verification.
  • IT service providers — form submissions are processed via Formspree under their privacy policy.
  • Regulatory authorities — where required by law or to comply with a lawful order.

06

Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy. Order and transaction records are retained for 7 years to comply with KRA requirements. Customer support correspondence is retained for 3 years. Analytics data is retained in aggregated, anonymised form indefinitely. On expiry of the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

07

Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data, request erasure of your data subject to our legal retention obligations, object to processing based on legitimate interests, withdraw consent for marketing at any time, and lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner of Kenya.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at orders@partsmtaani.com. We will respond within 21 days.

08

Cookies & Analytics

This portal uses Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymised usage data. GA4 uses cookies to distinguish users and report on site activity. No personally identifiable information is transmitted to Google Analytics. You may disable cookies through your browser settings without affecting your ability to submit a pre-order.

09

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration or destruction. The portal is served over HTTPS and protected by Cloudflare's security infrastructure. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure; while we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

11

Contact

For any privacy-related queries or to exercise your data rights, contact us by email at orders@partsmtaani.com or by phone on 011 987 3011.
