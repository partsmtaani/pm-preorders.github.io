1. Who We Are

Staydate Holdings Limited (trading as PartsMtaani) is the data controller for personal information collected through this Platform. Our registered address is in Nairobi, Kenya, and our KRA PIN is P051234567Z.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Full name and phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address and location
  • Vehicle details (make, model, year, engine code, VIN/chassis)
  • Part descriptions, OEM part numbers, reference images
  • Payment reference numbers (M-Pesa transaction IDs)
  • Communications you send us

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URLs
  • Device type

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from payment processors (Safaricom M-Pesa) to verify transactions.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Order fulfilment: Processing your pre-order, communicating sourcing updates, arranging delivery.
  • Payment processing: Verifying your payment and issuing invoices.
  • Customer support: Responding to queries, handling returns and refunds.
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our obligations under Kenyan law.
  • Service improvement: Understanding how customers use the Platform.
  • Fraud prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent orders.
  • Marketing (optional): Sending order updates and occasional promotions.

We will never use your vehicle details, VIN, or chassis information for any purpose other than fulfilling your specific order.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to fulfil a pre-order agreement.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with tax and regulatory requirements.
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention, service improvement, network security.
  • Consent: Marketing communications, where you have opted in.

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share your data only in limited circumstances:

  • Suppliers and logistics partners: Your name, delivery address, and order details.
  • Payment processors: Safaricom processes M-Pesa transactions.
  • Email and communication tools: Third-party services for order confirmations.
  • Legal authorities: Where required by Kenyan law.
  • Business transfer: If Staydate Holdings Limited is acquired or merged.

6. Data Retention

  • Order and payment records: 7 years (KRA requirement).
  • Customer support communications: 2 years.
  • Website analytics data: 26 months.
  • Marketing consent records: Until you withdraw consent.

7. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal data including TLS/HTTPS encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews.

8. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have rights to access, correction, deletion, objection, withdrawal of consent, data portability, and lodging a complaint with the ODPC.

9. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for function, analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4), and Cloudflare security cookies. No advertising cookies are used.

10. Children's Privacy

PartsMtaani is not intended for persons under 18 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy and will post the revised version on this page.
