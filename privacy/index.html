1. Who We Are

Staydate Holdings Limited (trading as PartsMtaani) is the data controller for personal information collected through this Platform. Our registered address is in Nairobi, Kenya, and our KRA PIN is P051234567Z.

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, please contact us using the details in Section 12.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a pre-order or contact us, we collect:

  • Full name and phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address and location
  • Vehicle details (make, model, year, engine code, VIN/chassis number)
  • Part descriptions, OEM part numbers, reference images you upload
  • Payment reference numbers (M-Pesa transaction IDs)
  • Communications you send us via email, WhatsApp, or phone

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect limited technical data:

  • IP address and approximate geolocation
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Referring URLs
  • Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)

This data is collected via server logs and, where enabled, Google Analytics 4. We do not use this data to build individual profiles for advertising purposes.

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from payment processors (Safaricom M-Pesa) to verify transactions. We do not purchase contact lists or obtain your data from data brokers.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order fulfilment: Processing your pre-order, communicating sourcing updates, arranging delivery.
  • Payment processing: Verifying your M-Pesa or bank payment and issuing invoices.
  • Customer support: Responding to queries, handling returns and refunds.
  • Legal compliance: Meeting our obligations under Kenyan law, including KRA tax records and the Data Protection Act.
  • Service improvement: Understanding how customers use the Platform to improve our processes and content.
  • Fraud prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent orders or abuse of the Platform.
  • Marketing (optional): Sending you order updates and, where you have opted in, occasional promotions. You can unsubscribe at any time.

We will never use your vehicle details, VIN, or chassis information for any purpose other than fulfilling your specific order and verifying part compatibility.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to fulfil a pre-order agreement with you.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with tax, KRA, and regulatory requirements.
  • Legitimate interests: Fraud prevention, service improvement, and network security.
  • Consent: Marketing communications, where you have explicitly opted in.

5. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Suppliers and logistics partners: Your name, delivery address, and order details are shared with the supplier and courier needed to fulfil your order.
  • Payment processors: Safaricom processes M-Pesa transactions under their own privacy policy.
  • Email and communication tools: We use third-party services (e.g., Formspree, Mailjet) to send order confirmations. These processors are contractually bound to handle your data securely.
  • Legal authorities: We may disclose your data to law enforcement or regulatory bodies where required by Kenyan law or a valid court order.
  • Business transfer: If Staydate Holdings Limited is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to the same protections under this Policy.

All third parties we share data with are required to handle it in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy, and in accordance with Kenyan legal requirements:

  • Order and payment records: 7 years (KRA statutory requirement).
  • Customer support communications: 2 years from last interaction.
  • Website analytics data: 26 months (GA4 default retention).
  • Marketing consent records: Until you withdraw consent, plus 1 year thereafter.

When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. These include:

  • TLS/HTTPS encryption on all data in transit
  • Access controls limiting staff access to data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular review of third-party processor security practices
  • Cloudflare DDoS and bot protection on the Platform

No system is completely secure. If you become aware of any security vulnerability or data breach relating to PartsMtaani, please notify us immediately at the address in Section 12.

8. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, subject to our legal retention obligations.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent for marketing at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Lodge a complaint: Complain to the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) at odpc.go.ke.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 12. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies

Our website uses a limited number of cookies. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies from third-party ad networks.

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website function (session management, form security). Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4, used to understand traffic patterns in aggregate. You can opt out via your browser settings or a GA4 opt-out browser add-on.
  • Cloudflare cookies: Used for DDoS protection and security by our CDN provider.

You can manage cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the site from functioning correctly.

10. Children's Privacy

PartsMtaani is not intended for persons under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a person under 18, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated Policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. For material changes, we will notify registered customers via email at least 14 days in advance.
