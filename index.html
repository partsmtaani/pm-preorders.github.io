Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about how pre-ordering works, the deposit, how sourcing works, and what happens when things don't go to plan.

We can source mechanical, electrical and body parts for most Japanese, German, Korean and American vehicles sold in Kenya. This includes engines, gearboxes, suspension components, ECUs, headlamps, body panels and more. If you're unsure whether we can help with a specific part, submit a request and we'll advise within 24 hours.
When you submit a request, we research current market prices and give you an estimated price range — e.g. KES 45,000–55,000. The deposit is 30% of the mid-point of that range (KES 15,000 in this example). It is credited in full toward your final invoice — not an additional fee. Sourcing only begins once the deposit is received.
If the confirmed price comes back more than 20% above our original estimate, you are entitled to a full refund of your deposit — no deductions, no questions asked. We own that error because the estimate is ours to get right. If the confirmed price is within 20% of the estimate, you decide whether to proceed or walk away; no refund applies if you decline a price that is within the agreed tolerance.
If we exhaust our supplier network and cannot find your part within the agreed sourcing window, your entire deposit is refunded in full within 3 business days — no deductions. We will always tell you clearly when we have reached that point rather than leave you waiting.
If you cancel after we have begun sourcing — whether or not the part has been found — we will refund your deposit in full less any documented out-of-pocket expenses we have incurred (supplier calls, yard visit fees and similar). These deductions are capped at USD 18 in total. We provide receipts for every deduction on request. The balance is returned to your M‑Pesa within 3 business days. Alternatively, we can hold the full amount as store credit with no deductions.
Light parts (sensors, ECUs, headlights, alternators) typically arrive within 3–5 days via air freight. Heavy parts — engines, gearboxes, body panels — come by sea and take 14–21 days to Mombasa plus onward delivery. The total lead time from deposit to delivery is typically 7–14 days for regular orders.
This applies to new and OEM parts only — used and graded parts are sold as-is and carry no warranty. If your new part develops a fault, report it to us within 14 days of delivery. The part must have been installed by a certified or qualified mechanic; we may ask for proof of that. Return shipping is at the customer's cost. Once we have assessed the returned part and confirmed the defect, we will source a replacement. If a replacement cannot be found, we will issue a full refund of the part payment.
