Sourcing Fee

When Is the Sourcing Fee Refunded?

The 30% sourcing fee is credited toward your final invoice — it is not an additional charge. It is fully refundable in one circumstance only: when PartsMtaani is unable to source the requested part within the agreed lead time.

Eligible
  • Part cannot be sourced within the agreed timeframe
  • Order cancelled by PartsMtaani
Not eligible
  • Customer cancels after sourcing commences
  • Customer declines confirmed price
  • Incorrect information provided by customer
  • Customer unreachable for follow-up

Part Payment

When Is a Part Payment Refunded?

Part payments may be refunded in the following circumstances, provided the issue is reported within the applicable window:

  • Incorrect part supplied — report within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Part damaged in transit — document at delivery and report immediately.
  • Confirmed incompatibility — a qualified mechanic must verify the part cannot fit the stated vehicle, and the incompatibility must not have been disclosed at sourcing.
  • Warranty defects — manufacturing defects reported within 6 months of delivery, with proof of correct installation.

Delivery charges are not refundable. Approved refunds are processed to the original M-Pesa number within 3–5 business days.

Policy

What Is Not Covered

  • Parts that have been installed, modified or used
  • Damage caused by customer handling after delivery
  • Normal wear and tear, misuse or incorrect installation
  • Orders where inaccurate vehicle or part information was provided
