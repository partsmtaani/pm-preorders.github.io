Sourcing Fee Refunds

The 30% sourcing deposit is credited toward your final invoice — not an additional charge. It is refundable in one circumstance only: when Parts Mtaani cannot source the requested part within the agreed lead time. If you cancel after we have located the part, only our actual out-of-pocket expenses (calls and yard visit fees) are deducted and the balance is refunded.

Situation Eligible
Part cannot be sourced within the agreed timeframe Yes
Order cancelled by Parts Mtaani Yes
Final confirmed price is more than 20% above the estimate Yes
Customer cancels after sourcing has commenced Partial
Customer declines the confirmed sourced price (within 20%) No
Inaccurate vehicle information was provided No
Customer was unreachable during the follow-up period No

Part Payment Refunds

Part payments may be refunded where the issue is reported within the applicable window and the part has not been installed or used.

Reason Reporting Window
Incorrect part supplied 48 hours of delivery
Part damaged in transit At point of delivery — document before accepting
Confirmed incompatibility (verified by qualified mechanic) 7 days of delivery
Warranty defect with proof of correct installation Within 6-month warranty period

Delivery charges are not refundable. Approved refunds are processed to the original M-Pesa number within 3–5 business days.

What Is Not Covered

No refund or return is available for parts that have been installed, modified or used; damage caused by the customer after delivery; normal wear and tear, misuse or incorrect installation; or orders where inaccurate vehicle or part information was provided at the time of ordering.
