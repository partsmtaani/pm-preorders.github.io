3.2 Order Confirmation

An order is confirmed only when PartsMtaani sends you a written confirmation (via email or WhatsApp) along with a sourcing invoice. Verbal acknowledgements do not constitute confirmation.

3.3 Payment Methods

We accept payment via M-Pesa (Paybill 4048209) and bank transfer. PayPal payments are accepted only after prior written agreement with our support team. All prices are quoted in Kenya Shillings (KES) unless otherwise stated.

3.4 Price Changes

Quoted prices are estimates based on prevailing market rates. Final prices may vary due to supplier pricing changes, import duty fluctuations, or foreign exchange movements. We will notify you of any material price change before requiring additional payment.

4. Sourcing Fee

A non-refundable sourcing fee of 30% of the quoted part value is charged upon order confirmation. This fee:

  • Covers our sourcing labour, supplier communications, and import-related logistics costs.
  • Is credited in full toward your final invoice — it is not an additional charge on top of the part price.
  • Is fully refunded if PartsMtaani is unable to source the requested part within the agreed timeframe.
  • Is not refunded if you cancel after sourcing has commenced.

Our guarantee: If we cannot find your part, you receive a 100% refund of the sourcing fee, no questions asked. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.
