Service Description

Parts Mtaani operates a pre-order sourcing service for automotive parts across Kenya. We act as an intermediary, sourcing quality automotive parts from verified supplier networks on behalf of customers who have been unable to locate the required parts through conventional retail channels.

We do not maintain a physical stock of parts. All parts are sourced to order upon receipt of a valid Pre-Order and the associated Sourcing Fee.

Placing an Order

A Pre-Order is initiated when you submit a completed pre-order form via this portal. Submission of a form does not constitute a binding contract. A binding agreement is formed only upon receipt and confirmation of your Pre-Order by Parts Mtaani, and receipt of the applicable Sourcing Fee payment.

You are responsible for the accuracy of all information provided in the Pre-Order, including vehicle specifications, part numbers and condition preferences. Parts Mtaani will not be liable for sourcing incorrect parts arising from inaccurate customer-supplied information.

We reserve the right to decline any Pre-Order at our sole discretion without stating a reason.

Sourcing Fee

A 30% Sourcing Fee is charged per Pre-Order and calculated on the mid-point of the estimated price range provided to the Customer. It is applied directly toward the final invoice — it is a commitment deposit, not an additional charge. Payment must be made via M‑Pesa to Paybill 4048209 before sourcing activity commences.

The Sourcing Fee is fully refundable in the following circumstances: where Parts Mtaani is unable to source the requested part within the agreed lead time; where Parts Mtaani cancels the order; or where the final confirmed price exceeds the original estimate by more than 20%.

Where a Customer cancels after sourcing has commenced but before the part has been located, Parts Mtaani will deduct only its verified out-of-pocket expenses (including international call costs and any logistics coordination fees) and refund the balance within 3 business days. Receipts for deductions are available on request.

In all other cases — including where the Customer declines a confirmed price that is within 20% of the original estimate, provides inaccurate vehicle information, or becomes unreachable during the sourcing window — the Sourcing Fee is retained in full.

Pricing & Payment

Budget estimates provided at Pre-Order submission are indicative only. Final pricing is confirmed upon completion of sourcing and communicated to the Customer before any further payment is required. The Customer is under no obligation to purchase if the confirmed price exceeds their stated budget; however the Sourcing Fee will not be refunded in such cases.

Full payment for sourced parts is due prior to delivery. Parts Mtaani accepts M‑Pesa and other payment methods as communicated at the time of invoicing.

Delivery

Typical lead times are 7–14 business days from confirmation of a Pre-Order. Lead times are estimates and may vary based on part availability, supplier location and logistics. Parts Mtaani will notify the Customer of any material delays.

Delivery is available across Kenya. Delivery charges are calculated at the point of invoicing based on the Customer's specified delivery location. Risk of loss or damage passes to the Customer upon delivery.

Warranty

All parts sourced through Parts Mtaani carry a six-month warranty against manufacturing defects from the date of delivery, subject to the following conditions:

  • The part has been installed by a qualified mechanic in accordance with the manufacturer's specifications.
  • The defect is not attributable to misuse, accident, neglect, unauthorised modification or normal wear and tear.
  • The Customer provides proof of purchase and a written description of the defect within the warranty period.

Warranty claims are handled at our discretion through repair, replacement or refund of the part value. The Sourcing Fee and any delivery charges are excluded from warranty remedies.

Restricted Items

Parts Mtaani will not source or facilitate the import of the following categories of items, regardless of how a request is submitted:

  • Airbags and airbag modules — pyrotechnic safety components are subject to strict import controls and require specialist handling.
  • Catalytic converters — regulated under Kenya Revenue Authority and environmental import controls due to precious metal content.
  • Seatbelt pretensioners and other explosive safety components — classified as dangerous goods under IATA and IMDG regulations.
  • Refrigerants and aerosols — regulated under Kenya's environmental and customs legislation.
  • Counterfeit or cloned parts — parts bearing false OEM markings or fraudulently represented as genuine manufacturer components.
  • Parts from vehicles with no valid documentation — Parts Mtaani will not source parts from vehicles with no verifiable provenance where required by the supplying yard's jurisdiction.
  • Any item prohibited under Kenyan customs law or the East African Community Customs Management Act — customers are responsible for verifying the import eligibility of any part before submitting a Pre-Order.

Parts Mtaani reserves the right to decline any request that, in its reasonable opinion, involves a restricted or borderline item. In such cases, the Sourcing Fee will be refunded in full.
